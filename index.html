Simone Biles

The Greatest Gymnast of All Time

Here are a few fun facts about Simone's accomplishments:

  • Simone is the most decorated gymnast ever with 30 World Championships medals
  • She particpated in the 2016 and 2020 Summer Olympics and has a combined 7 Olympic medals
  • Simone has a total of 5 skills named after her, two on floor, two on vault, and one on balance beam
